Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2025

This privacy policy describes how our game (“the App”) handles information when you use it. By using the App, you agree to this policy.

The App may show ads served through Google AdMob. AdMob and its partners may collect and use data to show and measure ads, including device identifiers, IP address, app usage, and similar technical or advertising-related information, in line with Google’s policies.

For details on how Google uses data when you use apps that integrate Google services, see Google’s privacy documentation and AdMob policies. You can adjust ad personalization on your device through your system or Google account settings where available.

Apple Game Center

If you sign in to or use Apple Game Center features (such as achievements or multiplayer), Apple processes account and gameplay-related data according to Apple’s Privacy Policy. We do not control Apple’s collection or use of that information.

Leaderboards stored on your device

Leaderboard and high-score data are stored locally on your device (for example, in app storage on your phone or tablet). This data stays on your device unless you choose to share it through features the App provides (such as Game Center). We do not operate a separate cloud leaderboard that we host for this local data by default.

If you uninstall the App or clear app data, locally stored leaderboard information may be deleted and cannot always be recovered.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age required by applicable law to provide consent for data collection without parental permission. If you believe we have collected information from a child inappropriately, please contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

Contact
