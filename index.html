🛒 In-App Purchases

Minesweeper offers the following optional purchases:

  • Extra Hints $1.99 — Adds 5 additional hints to your current game. Hints highlight safe cells to reveal or mines to flag.
  • AI Solver $2.99 — Unlocks the AI auto-solver that can play the game for you using advanced constraint-based algorithms.
  • Remove Ads $4.99 — Permanently removes all banner and interstitial advertisements from the app.

You can also earn coins by winning games and spend them on hints or to continue a game after losing.

🎮 How to Play

  • Tap a cell to reveal it
  • Long press a cell to place or remove a flag
  • Numbers show how many mines are adjacent to that cell
  • Tap a number to chord-reveal (auto-reveal neighbors when all mines are flagged)
  • Reveal all non-mine cells to win

Three difficulty levels: Easy (9×9, 10 mines), Medium (16×16, 40 mines), Hard (16×30, 99 mines).
